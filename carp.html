For this poster I wanted to try and keep a certain level of minimalism. For this reason I tried to keep a Monocromatic colourscheme with the addition of some Red accents which I think really pulls the design together. Overall I foud this poster fun to make but it took me a moment to get the ball rolling.

For this poster I took heavy inspiration from the pieces of De Stijl artist Piet Mondian. I have enjoyed his pieces and Theo van Doesburg's since the beginning of A levels and because of this they have become some of my favourite Modernists.

Finally, for my third poster I wanted try and capture an old feel. I took inspiration from early hot air balloons. I chose to used again a quite Monocromatic colourscheme and again used a red as an accent.
